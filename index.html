Dine On Campus!

Welcome to my Dine On Campus blog!

This blog was created to showcase resturants right around the UNCG campus! It includes resturants to try along with dish recommendations for each resturant. These are some amazing places to visit with friends or classmates! The dishes presented are all different from each other so that their is a good showcase of what each resturant has to offer.

The Upper Deck

The Upper Deck is a resturant that offers classic bar food and cocktails. Their play on American food draws in a large crowd along with many positive reviews. I have highlighted some showcase dishes below.

The operating hours are Sun-Wed. 11am - Midnight and Thurs-Sat. 11am - 1am.

Fire Drill Dirty Fries

As an eccentric starter, there are the Fire Drill Dirty Fries. Loaded with buffalo chicken, mac and cheese, ranch, pickles, and their own special "Deck" sauce. This is a dish you don't want to miss during football season!

Honey Heatwave Chicken Sandwich

The Honey Heatwave Chicken Sandwich is their play on a classic chicken sandwich. It is described as breaded chicken breast coated in hot honey, topped with jalepenos, pickles, and honey sauce, all stacked on a sweet potato bun. This is a must if you love a good chicken sandwich.

The Spartan Smash Burger is a dish I had to include for The Upper Deck. This is a sfour patty smash burger topped with the classic burger toppings, bacon, onion rings, and their "Deck" sauce. This burger is a beast and definently expresses the Spartan spirit!

Sushi Republic

The Sushi Republic is a sushi resturant right near campus that is described to offer various unique sushi rolls and Japanese fusion dishes. The menu prices are reasonable for the food you get, and portion sizes are great.

The operating hours are Mon-Friday 11:30am - 2:30pm and 5:00pm - 9:30pm, Sat. they are only open 5pm - 10pm. They are closed Sunday.

Spicy Crab Salad Roll

This is a sushi roll filled with spicy crab, avocado, and cucumber. It is topped with redsnapper, Japanese mayo, and spicy eel sauce. Perfect for those who love heat!

Beef Bulgogi tacos

The Beef Bulgogi tacos are classic flour tortillas filled with Korean beef BBQ and topped with cilantro, pico, and avocado. This is a fusion between Asian and Mexican foods, and the meat can be substituted for pork or red snapper too. Perfect for those craving a unique but delicious combination.

Chicken Udon

The Chicken Udon is a classic dish of udon noodles topped with chicken, carrots, onion, zucchini, and cabbage. For those who want a simple but perfect classic dish.

Crazy Mexico

Crazy Mexico is a vibrant and traditional Mexican resturant that brings fresh authentic Mexican food to Tate St. This is perfect for any time of the day and a great recommendation for going out with friends.

Nachos Supreme

This is a nacho dish where the chips are covered in ground beef, chicken, refriend beans, lettuce, tomato, and sour cream. This is a great starter or light lunch food.

Burrito Hawaiano

The Burrito Hawaino is a flour tortilla filled with chicken, steak, shrimp, pineapple, onions, peppers, and tomatos. The burrito is then topped with queso, bacon, and a pineapple ring. This is a great unique dish mixing pineapple with traditional Mexican flavors.

Crazy Quesadilla

The Crazy Quesadilla is a flour tortilla filled with shrimp, cheese, peppers, onions, and tomato. It is served with a guacamole salad, sour cream, and a side of rice. Perfect for a classic but delicious dish.
